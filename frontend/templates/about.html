Common Service Centres 2.0 Scheme

Based on the assessment of Common Service Centres scheme, the Government launched the Common Service Centres 2.0 scheme in 2015 to expand the outreach of Common Service Centres to all Gram Panchayats across the country.
Under Common Service Centres 2.0 scheme, at least one Common Service Centres will be set up in each of the 2.5 lakh GPs across the country by 2019. Common Service Centres functioning under the existing scheme will also be strengthened and integrated with additional 1.5 lakh Common Service Centres across the country.
Common Service Centres 2.0 scheme would consolidate service delivery through a universal technology platform, thereby making e-services, particularly G2C services accessible to citizens anywhere in the country.

Key Features of Common Service Centres 2.0 scheme

  1. A self-sustaining network of 2.5 lakh Common Service Centres in Gram Panchayats
  2. Large bouquet of e-services through a single delivery platform
  3. Standardization of services and capacity building of stakeholders
  4. Localised Help Desk support
  5. Sustainability of VLEs through maximum commission sharing
  6. Encouraging more women as VLEs
